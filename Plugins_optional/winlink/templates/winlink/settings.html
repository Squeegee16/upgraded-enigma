About Winlink

Winlink is a worldwide radio email system for amateur radio operators.

This plugin uses Pat - a native Linux Winlink client.

  • Telnet: Internet gateway
  • AX.25: VHF/UHF packet
  • VARA HF: HF digital
  • VARA FM: VHF digital
  • ARDOP: HF digital
