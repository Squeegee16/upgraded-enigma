Antenna Tips

VHF (137 MHz) - NOAA/METEOR:
Turnstile or QFH antenna recommended.

L-Band (1.7 GHz):
Patch or helix antenna required.
LNA strongly recommended.

S-Band (2.4 GHz):
Dish antenna required.
